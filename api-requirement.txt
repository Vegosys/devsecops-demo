Loan service - what we need
---------------------------

Hi team,

We want to build a simple online service for our loan product. Right now everything
runs on email and spreadsheets and it's becoming a mess.

Here's what it should do:

- A customer should be able to apply for a loan online. They'll give us their name and
  how much they want to borrow.
- After they apply, we (and they) should be able to look the application up later and see
  where it stands - still being reviewed, approved, or rejected.
- Our ops team needs to check the status of any application quickly.

Things that matter to us:

- It has to be secure. This is people's financial information - we cannot have data leaking.
- It should be fast and shouldn't go down, especially during business hours.
- We have a SOC 2 audit coming up, so whatever we build needs to keep us compliant.
- Please don't store or log people's personal details in plain text anywhere.

We don't have strong opinions on the technology - that's your call. Just make it solid
and easy to run.

Loan amounts are usually between ten thousand and a few crore. Anything outside a sensible
range should just be rejected.

Thanks,
Priya (Product)
